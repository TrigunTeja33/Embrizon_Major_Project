Event Features

Explore the highlights of TechVolution 2024.

Keynote Speeches

Keynote speeches at TechVolution 2024 will feature talks by industry leaders and visionaries who will share their insights on the future of technology. These speeches aim to inspire and provide valuable perspectives on emerging trends and innovations.

Startup Pitch Competition

The Startup Pitch Competition offers a platform for innovative startups to present their ideas to a panel of judges, including investors and industry leaders. This competition encourages entrepreneurship and innovation, providing startups with exposure and potential funding opportunities.

Testing Competition

The Testing Competition adds an interactive element to TechVolution 2024, challenging participants to identify and solve bugs in provided code. Competitors will be given code snippets or applications with intentional bugs, and they must debug and fix them within a specified time frame.

Quiz Competition on Technology Trends

The Quiz Competition on Technology Trends offers attendees an opportunity to test their knowledge of the latest advancements and trends in the tech industry. Participants will be quizzed on topics such as AI, cybersecurity, IoT, and emerging technologies, challenging them to stay updated and informed.

Panel-Tech Exhibition

The Tech Exhibition features the latest tech products and innovations. Attendees can explore various booths throughout the day, engaging with cutting-edge technologies. The exhibition will be showcased using a gallery with images of participating exhibitors, making it easy for attendees to navigate and explore. The Tech Exhibition will feature interactive tech demos where attendees can experience the latest technologies firsthand.

Interactive Workshops

Interactive workshops offering attendees hands-on sessions on cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain, and Quantum Computing. These workshops are designed to provide practical experience and in-depth knowledge from industry experts

Event Timeline

Our journey through the years.

2013
TechVolution was founded.

TechVolution was conceived as a visionary initiative by InnovateTech Expo. With a vision to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange within the tech community, TechVolution embarked on a journey to connect industry professionals, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts.

2018
First international conference held in Berlin.

TechVolution expanded its reach by organizing its first international conference in Berlin. This marked a significant milestone in TechVolution's journey, as it attracted participants and speakers from around the globe. The conference served as a catalyst for cross-border collaboration and showcased the global impact of technology.

2021
Virtual event reaches over 10,000 participants.

Amidst the challenges posed by the global pandemic, TechVolution adapted by hosting its first virtual event in 2020. Despite the shift to an online format, the event successfully attracted over 10,000 participants from various corners of the world. This virtual gathering demonstrated TechVolution's resilience and commitment to fostering innovation and learning, even in challenging times.

2023
Celebrating a decade of technological evolution.<

TechVolution celebrated its tenth anniversary, marking a decade of technological evolution and progress. As TechVolution looks ahead to the future, it remains committed to driving positive change and empowering the next generation of technology leaders.
